### Mermaid

Diagrams with mermaid, make sure it is enabled in the `_config.yml`.
Here is a simple example:

```html
<!-- To generate a diagram -->
<div class="mermaid">
sequenceDiagram
    Alice->>John: Hello John, how are you?
    John-->>Alice: Great!
</div>
```
